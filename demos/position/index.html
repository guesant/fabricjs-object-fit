Absolute Point

  • x: Point.fromAbsolute(10)
  • y: Point.fromAbsolute(10)

Percentage

  • x: Point.fromPercentage("50%")
  • y: Point.fromPercentage("75%")

Aliased

It uses Point.fromTag() under the hood.

  • x: Point.X.RIGHT
  • y: Point.Y.CENTER

Tagged

Point.Tag is an enum. The supported values are: Point.Tag.START, Point.Tag.CENTER and Point.Tag.END.

  • x: Point.fromTag(TAG.START)
  • y: Point.fromTag(TAG.END)

Factor

Formula:

f(ContainerSize, ObjectSize, factor) = (ContainerSize - ObjectSize) * factor

It's like the percentage mode, but without the division by 100.

  • x: Point.fromFactor(0.5)
  • y: Point.fromFactor(1)
